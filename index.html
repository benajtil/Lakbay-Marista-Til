    Travel Memories

    Kamayadan Festival

    Photographer: Nanardx
    Every month of March the Municipality of Norala in South Cotabato celebrates the Kamayadan Festival, a colorful and joyful celebration of the town’s rich agricultural harvests, governance’s feats, and a showcasing of the people’s culture and traditions.

    T'nalak Festival

    Photographer: Nanardx
    Every month of July, the vibrant province of South Cotabato, nestled in the heart of Mindanao, comes alive with a spectacular burst of colors, beats of traditional music, and the rich aroma of local cuisine. This is the T'nalak Festival, a celebration like no other, paying homage to South Cotabato's rich culture and history, and the beautiful handwoven fabric - the T'nalak of the Tboli's.

    T'boli Illuminated

    Photographer: Denn Jib Seblos
    This photo was taken during the culmination of the Seslong Festival 2019. The foreground clearly shows TBOLI KNOON, simply put, it means beautiful T'Boli. It serves as a greeting to visitors and tourists, and also a reminder to the locals. The fireworks were just added bonus to the already spectacular scene.

    Mt. Matutum and the Hypnotic Stars

    Photographer: Ramil Leonida
    Mt. Matutum had been the subject of many landscape photos from sunrise til sunset. During the night, its also one of the best subject while shooting for the stars in the night sky.

    Tunnel of Lights

    Photographer: Aleli Faith Cabahug
    Step into a world of enchantment at the Kasadya Christmas Village in Surallah, South Cotabato, where the Tunnel of Lights ignites the spirit of the season with its mesmerizing glow. Experience the magic and make memories that sparkle forever.

    Kayaking is more fun at Lake Holon

    Photographer: Jeniel Buday
    Enjoy the fresh air and lush green sceneries while paddling in the crystal clear water of majestic Lake Holon. Kayaking is another outdoor activity attraction offered for campers and guests of Lake Holon in Tboli, South Cotabato.
